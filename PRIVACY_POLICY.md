# Privacy Policy — FlickPickr

**Last updated: March 2026**

FlickPickr ("the app", "we", "us") is operated by FlickPickr as an individual developer. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and your rights regarding it.

---

## 1. What We Collect

### Account information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Your **email address** — used to authenticate you and communicate account-related information
- Your **display name** (username) — shown to other members of groups you join

### Usage data
While you use the app, we store:
- Your **swipe history** — which movies and TV shows you liked, loved, or passed on. This is the core data that powers the app's matching and recommendation features.
- Your **group memberships** — which groups you have created or joined, and the invite codes associated with them

### What we do NOT collect
- We do not collect location data
- We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- We do not track you across other apps or websites

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Email address | Sign in, password reset, account security |
| Display name | Shown to group members you share a group with |
| Swipe history | Calculating matches within your groups; powering recommendations |
| Group memberships | Showing you your groups and enabling the invite and match flow |

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The app relies on the following external services:

**Supabase** (database and authentication)
All account and swipe data is stored in a Supabase Postgres database. Supabase infrastructure is hosted in the EU/US. See [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

**The Movie Database (TMDB)**
Movie and TV show data (titles, posters, descriptions, ratings) is fetched from the TMDB API. Requests are made from your device directly to TMDB's servers. We do not send any personal data to TMDB. See [themoviedb.org/privacy-policy](https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy).

**YouTube (via Google)**
Trailers are played using an embedded YouTube player. When you tap "Watch Trailer", YouTube may collect data as described in [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for their own purposes.

Data is only shared in the following limited circumstances:
- **Within your groups**: your display name and swipe history are visible to other members of groups you have joined, solely to enable the matching feature
- **Legal requirements**: if required by law, we may disclose data to comply with a legal obligation

---

## 5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists.

**To delete your account and all associated data:**
Go to the Profile tab inside the app and tap **Delete Account**. This permanently removes your email, display name, swipe history, and group memberships from our systems.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

FlickPickr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
